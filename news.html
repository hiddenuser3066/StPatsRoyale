St Pats Royale – Update v1.0.3

Server Status

We’re rolling out new balance changes and improvements to prepare for upcoming features.

Gameplay Changes
  • Knight now summons 2 Skeletons every 3 seconds while alive.
  • P.E.K.K.A now spawns a weakened Mini P.E.K.K.A on defeat (50% HP, 60% damage).
  • Archers now fire 2 arrows per attack with a slight horizontal spread.
  • Bomber now triggers a small explosion on death for added impact.
  • Baby Dragon now emits a 2-tile burning aura dealing 50 damage every 0.5s.
  • Cannon now fires a 3-shot burst instead of a single projectile.
  • Witch now summons Bats instead of Skeletons.
  • Giant now creates a 3-tile damaging blast dealing 300 damage on defeat.
  • Goblin Hut now spawns 3 Goblins every 1.5 seconds and lasts 30 seconds.
  • Barbarians buffed: Speed increased to 90.0, Damage increased to 80.0.
General Improvements
  • Improved match sync and networking stability.
  • New deck loadout interface for smoother card switching.
  • Minor UI polish and visual refinements.
  • General bug fixes and small balance tweaks.
